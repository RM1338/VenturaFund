Our Story

Heavy rains and landslides have trapped dozens in remote villages. This fund supports urgent rescue operations, food drops, and medical evacuation for people stranded and injured by the natural disaster.

We're collaborating with local authorities, mountain rescue teams, and volunteers to reach those in danger and bring essential supplies where roads are cut off by landslides.

How Your Donation Helps

  • Helicopter rescue missions and fuel
  • Emergency food and water supplies
  • First-aid and medical evacuation for the injured
  • Tents, dry clothing, and basic survival kits
  • Communication equipment for rescue teams

Together, we can save lives and bring relief to those cut off by disaster. Every donation directly powers rescue efforts.
