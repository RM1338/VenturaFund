Our Story

Severe floods have devastated hundreds of families in Assam. We're raising funds to provide urgent food, medical supplies, and shelter to those left homeless by the recent disaster.

Our team is on the ground distributing relief materials and coordinating with local NGOs for efficient delivery to the worst-hit areas. We need your support to sustain and expand relief operations during this critical time.

How Funds Will Be Used

  • Emergency food and clean water supplies
  • Medical kits and hygiene products
  • Tents, blankets, and clothing
  • Rescue transportation and logistics
  • Support for rebuilding basic infrastructure

Your contribution can save lives and help families recover from disaster. Every rupee counts!

Thank you for caring and helping us restore hope in Assam.
