Our Story

A powerful earthquake has left hundreds homeless and in urgent need of food, shelter, and medical attention in Uttarkashi. This fund provides critical aid to affected families and helps them recover and rebuild.

We are working with local authorities and rescue teams to deliver emergency relief and ensure no one is left behind during this crisis.

How Funds Will Be Used

  • Temporary shelters and safe accommodation
  • Emergency food and clean water distribution
  • Medical help and first-aid for injured
  • Child and elderly care
  • Initial help with house repairs and supplies
