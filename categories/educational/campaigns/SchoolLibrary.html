Build a School Library in Rural Community

M
Maria Garcia
Hillsboro, Texas

Our Story

My name is Maria Garcia, and I'm a retired elementary school teacher on a mission to bring the first library to our rural farming community in Hillsboro, Texas.

I taught here for 35 years and watched generations of children grow up without access to books. Our community is home to over 400 school-aged children, yet the nearest library is 45 miles away‚Äîan impossible distance for families working long hours on farms and ranches.

These children deserve the same opportunities as kids in cities. They deserve to discover the magic of reading, to explore worlds beyond our small town, and to develop a lifelong love of learning. A library can change everything.

The Vision

We've secured a beautiful 2,000 square-foot space in the center of town‚Äîdonated by a generous local business owner. Now we need to transform this empty building into a vibrant community library that will serve children and families for generations to come.

The library will feature:

  • A children's reading corner with comfortable seating and colorful decor
  • 5,000+ books spanning all reading levels and interests
  • Study tables and quiet reading nooks
  • After-school tutoring space
  • Computer stations for homework and research
  • Community meeting room for book clubs and literacy programs

How Funds Will Be Used

Your generous donations will cover:

  • Books & Materials ($12,000): Age-appropriate books, reference materials, and educational resources
  • Furniture & Shelving ($7,000): Library shelves, reading tables, chairs, and children's furniture
  • Technology ($4,000): Computers, cataloging software, and internet setup
  • Renovation & Decor ($2,000): Paint, lighting, and creating an inviting atmosphere

I've watched students struggle without access to books for homework. I've seen talented readers plateau because they couldn't find new books at their level. I've counseled parents who desperately wanted to help their children but had no resources.

This library will change that. It will give every child in our community a fair shot at success.

We're 73% of the way to our goal! With your support, we'll open the doors to this library by summer, just in time for our summer reading program. Every dollar brings us closer to giving these children the gift of literacy.

Thank you for believing in the power of books and the potential of rural children. Together, we're building more than a library‚Äîwe're building dreams.
