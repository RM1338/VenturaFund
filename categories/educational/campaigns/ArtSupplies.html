Our Story

I'm Sarah Miller, an art teacher and nonprofit founder. I'm raising funds to provide art supplies for our after-school program serving at-risk youth in Portland, Oregon.

Creativity is a lifeline for the children in our community. Many come from families facing homelessness, addiction, or economic hardship. Our after-school art program gives them a safe space to be expressive, to heal, and to dream.
